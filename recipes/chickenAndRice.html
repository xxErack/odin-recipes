Easy Chicken and Rice


Description

  • This chicken and rice recipe is simple, filling, and comforting.
  • It’s a one-pan style meal that’s perfect for beginners.
  • Great for meal prep and reheats well.
  • Balanced with protein, carbs, and flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or breasts
  • White rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt & pepper
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Parsley (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sear chicken until golden brown.
  3. Remove chicken and sauté onions and garlic in the same pan.
  4. Add rice and stir for 1–2 minutes.
  5. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  6. Place chicken back on top of rice.
  7. Cover and cook on low until rice is tender and chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes, then fluff and serve.
