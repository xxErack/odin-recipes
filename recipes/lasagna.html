Easy Homemade Lasagna


Description

  • This recipe has been called the best by many and has thousands of 5-star reviews
  • It uses simple ingredients that you likely have on hand, and it packs big flavor!
  • This is an easy lasagna recipe. It requires just one pan, one bowl, and a 9x13 baking dish.
  • It reheats well and can be made ahead of time. You can freeze lasagna either before or after baking.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook ground beef with garlic, salt, and pepper until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain.
  5. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers until dish is full, finishing with cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
