Easy Homemade Pizza


Description

  • This homemade pizza is simple, fast, and packed with flavor.
  • You can customize it with your favorite toppings.
  • Perfect for beginners learning basic cooking skills.
  • Great for family nights, game nights, or solo snack attacks.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni or any toppings you like.
  6. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and season.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes, slice, and serve.
